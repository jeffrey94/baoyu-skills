## Text Hierarchy

Recommended text sizing for visual hierarchy. IG slides use fewer hierarchy levels than text-heavy platforms.

| Level | Role | Relative Size | Style | Words Target |
|-------|------|---------------|-------|--------------|
| H1 | Main title/headline | 100% | Bold, impactful | 3-8 words |
| H2 | Section header | 60-70% | Semi-bold | 2-5 words |
| Body | Supporting text | 40-50% | Regular | 5-15 words per point |
| Caption | Small notes, credits | 25-30% | Light | As needed |

**Key principle**: One level of emphasis per slide. If the headline is bold, body text should be understated.

## Style-Specific Typography

### Clean-Minimal Style
- Clean sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica feel)
- Structured hierarchy with consistent tracking
- Minimal decoration, let whitespace breathe

### Bold-Graphic Style
- Impactful sans-serif with 3D shadows
- High contrast, large scale headlines
- Neon or vibrant color accents on text

### Editorial Style
- Mixed serif + sans-serif pairing
- Magazine-quality hierarchy
- Refined letter-spacing and kerning

### Soft-Muted Style
- Rounded sans-serif or gentle serif
- Soft shadows, muted text colors
- Warm, approachable letterforms

### Line-Art Style
- Clean hand-lettering or monoline
- Single consistent stroke weight
- Minimal, educational clarity
