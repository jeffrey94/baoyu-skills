# Retro-Nostalgia Style

Warm 70s tones, Y2K pastels, film grain — trendy and cultural.

## Element Combination

```yaml
canvas:
  ratio: portrait-4-5
  grid: single | dual
```

## Visual Elements

- Film grain overlay on everything
- Vintage badges, retro shapes
- Polaroid frames, film strip borders
- Aged texture overlays (noise, halftone)
- Uneven baselines, imperfect alignment (intentional)

## Typography

- Retro display fonts (70s rounded, Y2K pixel, groovy script)
- Uneven baselines for authenticity
- Distorted or warped text effects
- Mixed sizes for dynamic feel
