# Soft-Muted Style

Muted pastels, desaturated tones, soft gradients — dreamy and gentle.

## Element Combination

```yaml
canvas:
  ratio: portrait-4-5
  grid: single | dual

image_effects:
  cutout: soft
  stroke: white-solid
  filter: soft-glow | desaturated-matte

typography:
  decorated: highlight | gradient
  tags: pill | underline-accent
  direction: horizontal

decorations:
  emphasis: underline | dot-bullet
  background: neutral-warm | gradient-linear
  elements: organic-blobs | minimal-lines
  frames: rounded-rect | glass-panel
```

## Color Palette

| Role | Colors | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Primary | Warm gray, soft charcoal | #6B6B6B, #4A4A4A |
| Background | Blush pink, soft cream, lavender | #FDF2F0, #FFF8F0, #F0EEF8 |
| Accents | Dusty rose, sage green, muted gold | #D4A5A5, #A8C0A8, #D4C49A |

## Visual Elements

- Organic blob shapes, soft edges
- Gentle gradients, no harsh lines
- Soft shadows, frosted glass effects
- Pastel color washes
- Flowing, feminine compositions

## Typography

- Rounded sans-serif or gentle serif
- Soft text colors (never pure black)
- Light-weight body text
- Gentle emphasis (highlights, underlines)
