# Playful-Pop Style

Vibrant primaries, unexpected pairings, rounded shapes — energetic and fun.

## Element Combination

```yaml
canvas:
  ratio: portrait-4-5
  grid: single | dual | quad

image_effects:
  cutout: clean
  stroke: colored-solid | white-solid
  filter: none

typography:
  decorated: shadow-3d | stroke-text
  tags: pill | circle-badge | ribbon
  direction: horizontal | diagonal

decorations:
  emphasis: numbering | star-burst | checkmark
  background: solid-saturated | gradient-linear
  elements: confetti | stars-sparkles | geometric-shapes

```

- Bold rounded shapes, chunky elements
