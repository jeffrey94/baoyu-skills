# Bold-Graphic Style

Deep black background, neon/vibrant accents, high contrast — attention-grabbing.

## Element Combination

```yaml
canvas:
  ratio: portrait-4-5
  grid: single | dual

image_effects:
  cutout: clean
  stroke: colored-solid | glow
  filter: high-contrast

typography:
  decorated: shadow-3d | stroke-text | neon
  tags: black-white | ribbon
  direction: horizontal | diagonal

decorations:
  emphasis: arrow-minimal | numbering
  background: solid-dark | gradient-linear
  elements: geometric-shapes | stars-sparkles
  frames: none | shadow-card
```

## Color Palette

| Role | Colors | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Primary | Pure white, neon yellow | #FFFFFF, #F7FF00 |
| Background | Deep black, dark charcoal | #000000, #0D0D0D |
| Accents | Electric blue, hot pink, neon green | #00D4FF, #FF2D7B, #39FF14 |

## Visual Elements

- Strong geometric forms, sharp angles
- High contrast everything
- Neon glow effects on key elements
- Bold, commanding compositions
- 3D text shadows for depth

## Typography

- Impactful bold sans-serif with 3D shadows
- Large scale headlines (one word per line)
- Neon glow on key phrases
- High contrast text on dark backgrounds
