# Earthy-Organic Style

Terracotta, olive, warm tan, natural textures — grounded and authentic.

## Element Combination

| Accents | Terracotta, olive, warm tan, sage | #C1714F, #6B7F4E, #D4B896, #9CAF88 |

## Visual Elements

- Botanical illustrations (leaves, branches)
- Paper and linen textures
- Brush stroke accents
- Organic blob shapes (not geometric)
- Hand-crafted, imperfect aesthetic
- Warm, natural color washes

## Typography

- Organic hand-lettering or brush-script for headlines
- Clean serif for body text
- Warm text colors (brown, forest green — never pure black)
- Natural, relaxed spacing
