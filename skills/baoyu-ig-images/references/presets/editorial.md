# Editorial Style

Neutral palette, dramatic contrasts, mixed serif+sans — magazine-quality premium feel.

## Element Combination

```yaml
canvas:
  ratio: portrait-4-5
  grid: single | dual | triptych

image_effects:
  cutout: clean
  stroke: none | white-solid
  filter: high-contrast | cinematic-teal-orange

typography:
  decorated: mixed-weight | knockout
  tags: box-outline | underline-accent
  direction: horizontal | vertical

decorations:
  emphasis: underline | bracket
  background: solid-white | solid-dark | neutral-warm
  elements: editorial-frames | minimal-lines
  frames: thin-outline | rounded-rect
```

## Color Palette

| Role | Colors | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Primary | Black, warm white | #1A1A1A, #FAF8F5 |
| Background | Off-white, charcoal (alternating) | #F5F2EE, #2C2C2C |
| Accents | Burnt sienna, deep burgundy, or single accent | #C4725A, #722F37, #B8860B |

## Visual Elements

- Magazine-style layout rules (thin lines, columns)
- Dramatic black & white with single color accent
- Editorial frames and containers
- Sophisticated dividers and spacing
- Pull-quote style text treatments

## Typography

- Mixed serif + sans-serif pairing
- Serif for headlines, sans-serif for body (or vice versa)
- Magazine-quality hierarchy and kerning
- Elegant letter-spacing
- Vertical text for decorative accents
