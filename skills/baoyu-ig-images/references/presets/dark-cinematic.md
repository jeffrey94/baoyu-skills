# Dark-Cinematic Style

Dark charcoal/navy background, jewel tone accents — dramatic and premium.

## Element Combination

```yaml
canvas:
  ratio: portrait-4-5
  grid: single | dual

image_effects:
  cutout: clean
  stroke: glow | shadow
  filter: cinematic-teal-orange | high-contrast

typography:
  decorated: gradient | mixed-weight
  tags: box-outline | underline-accent
  direction: horizontal | vertical

decorations:
  emphasis: underline | bracket
  background: solid-dark | gradient-linear
  elements: minimal-lines | geometric-shapes
  frames: thin-outline | glass-panel
```

## Color Palette

| Role | Colors | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Primary | Pure white, warm gold | #FFFFFF, #D4A843 |
| Background | Deep charcoal, dark navy, near-black | #1A1A1A, #0D1B2A, #0A0A0A |
| Accents | Emerald, ruby, sapphire, amber | #2D7D46, #9B2335, #1E4D8C, #C4925A |

## Visual Elements

- Dramatic lighting, spotlight effects
- Jewel-tone color pops on dark canvas
- Elegant thin rules and borders
- Cinematic vignette on edges
- Luxurious, moody atmosphere
- Minimal decoration — let contrast speak

## Typography

- Elegant serif for headlines (Playfair feel)

- Dramatic contrast (white/gold on dark)
- Generous letter-spacing on headlines
- Vertical text for decorative accents
